🎲 World & Backstory Selector

At the beginning of Generation One, your Sim is in exile. Roll 1d3 or click below to discover their past and where they've ended up:

🎲 1 – Willow Creek: The Ashes of a Lie

The fire was no accident. You smelled the gasoline before the flames took hold, but no one believed you. You disappeared into the swamp, building your life out of rot and ruin, far from the world that failed you.

🎲 2 – Oasis Springs: The Exile’s Truth

You were cut out and cast away. In the desert, blistered and alone, you found something better than forgiveness—freedom.

🎲 3 – Newcrest: The Promise That Collapsed

A failed government program left you with an empty lot and a head full of hope. You’re not building what they promised. You’re building what they couldn’t.

🏚️ Lot Setup

  • Start with §0
  • Bulldozed residential lot
  • No furniture or structures
  • No cheats

🛠️ Required Lot Traits:

  • ✅ Off the Grid
  • 🔸 1 Challenge Trait: Filthy, Grody, Quake Zone, Volcanic Activity, Creepy Crawlies
  • 🔹 1 Helpful Trait: Homey, Sunny Aspect, Great Soil, Fast Internet (if earned)
